Hacking

Hacking is in three types:

1. White hat hacking

2.Black hat hacking

3.Grey hat hacking


white hat hacker:

White hat hacker also know as the ethical hacker who is in a good side of hacking like helping company or individual to get rid of geting hacked by bad hackers. white hat is an ethical security hacker. Ethical hacking is a term meant to imply a broader category than just penetration testing. Under the owner's consent, white-hat hackers aim to identify any vulnerabilities the current system has

Black hat hacking:

A black hat is a computer hacker who usually violates laws or typical ethical standards. The term originates from 1950s westerns, when bad guys typically wore black hats and good guys white hats. Black hat hackers aim to hack into any system for their own profit or out of malice.
